So they do what any of us would do... break into their home to find proof of the dastardly deed.<br /><br />So many critics charged that Hanks tarnished his reputation with this effort. But for the umpteenth time, the critics were just being overly critical. THE &#39;BURBS is a hilarious, well-written and wonderfully creative piece of work. It&#39;s the kind of movie Joe Dante (GREMLINS, Matin&eacute;e) does best.
