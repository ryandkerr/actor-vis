        <h4>Trivia</h4>

        <h4>Goofs</h4>
The calf seen wandering in the barricades scene is a whitefaced hereford poll. That breed did not exist before the 1880, and did not reach France until the 20th Century.        <a href="trivia?tab=gf&ref_=tt_trv_gf"
