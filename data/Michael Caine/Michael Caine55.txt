Great shots of New York (specifically the city&#39;s architecture), great situations, great parties and great music<br /><br />Among the Manhattan-dwelling characters is Michael Caine, who is married to Hannah (Mia Farrow) but lusts after her sister (Barbara Hershey) who lives with a tormented artist (Max Von Sydow.) Hannah&#39;s ex-husband (Woody Allen) starts dating her other sister (Diane Weist) who wants to date Sam Waterston, even though he&#39;d rather date her friend (Carrie Fisher).<br /><br />In addition to the cameo by Julia Louise Dreyfuss, the film features two supporting performances by old school actors, Lloyd Nolan and Mia Farrow&#39;s real life mom, the original Jane in the Tarzan movies, Maureen O&#39;Sullivan.<br /><br />If that&#39;s not enough, Allen throws in plenty of his trademark hypochondria hysteria, questioning the meaning of the universe and whether God exists.</p>
