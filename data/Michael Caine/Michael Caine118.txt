In the late 1950s John Mills was a guest on the JACK PAAR SHOW and spoke of how life imitated art in that a British submarine was lost in the North Sea under very similar circumstances to those portrayed in the film between the completion of shooting and release in the UK.  He said there was criticism in the British press at the time for it's release.<br /><br />I wish it was available on VHS or DVD in the Unites States, but I haven't been able to find it.  I would love to see it again.</p>
