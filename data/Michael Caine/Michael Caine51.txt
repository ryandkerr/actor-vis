                    <div>
                        <p itemprop="reviewBody">I notice that Netflix has this film listed under &quot;Thrillers.&quot; It&#39;s not a thriller, but rather a fairly complex drama about what happens when people who are schooled not to trust anyone have almost unlimited power with no accountability. There&#39;s never a &quot;stupid moment&quot; in this film that reveals to us, the audience, or to Michael Caine as the protagonist, who the bad guys are or what they&#39;ve done. We have to peel the layers off the onion along with Michael Caine. There is no James Bond ending in which the bad guys&#39; island explodes and collapses into the sea. Instead, there is a real world ending tailored for adults. I liked the film.</p>
