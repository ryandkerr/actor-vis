                    <div>
                        <p itemprop="reviewBody"> &quot;Quicksand&quot;, a film directed by the man who made the mighty &quot;Long Good Friday&quot;, is about a million miles away from that film. It is an action movie in the vein of &quot;The Fugitive&quot;, messily made with an average result from the two lead actors. While the movie has nothing much going for it, after renting it on DVD I must confess that I watched it 'till the end and enjoyed it's<br /><br />trashiness. So... if you've got too much spare time (like I do) and feel like a nice, B-movie night at home, you could do much worse than renting &quot;Quicksand&quot;. Just don't expect another &quot;Long Good Friday&quot;, maybe a &quot;Short Trashy Monday&quot;.</p>
