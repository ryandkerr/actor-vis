A young superstar (<a href="/name/nm2955013?ref_=tt_stry_pl">Liam Hemsworth</a>), seduced by unlimited wealth and power falls between them, and becomes trapped in the middle of the twists and turns of their life-and-death game of corporate espionage. By the time he realizes his life is in danger, he is in far too deep and knows far too much for them to let him walk away.                <em class="nobr">Written by
