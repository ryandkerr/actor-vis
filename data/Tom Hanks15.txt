        <h4>Trivia</h4>


In the French version of the movie, Dolores Torres says to Viktor that she goes to Star Trek conventions dressed as Uhura. In France, the Star Trek series is not as well known as in America and it was thought that the character of Yeoman Rand was less well known than the principal crew of the starship. 5 years later <a href="/name/nm0757855?ref_=tt_trv_trv">Zoe Saldana</a>, who plays Torres, would appear as Uhura in <a href="/title/tt0796366?ref_=tt_trv_trv">Star Trek</a> (2009) and its sequels.        <a href="trivia?ref_=tt_trv_trv"
class="nobr" >See more</a>  &raquo;

So all the cool tones are evident until Viktor starts to settle into his new home&#133; He is going to be stuck in New York&#39;s JFK airport for an unspecified amount of time&#133; From this moment we see Viktor stuck, trapped, unable to enter United States and that&#39;s the fun of this film&#133; <br /><br />Tom Hanks is really so calm, so likable, so emotional, so funny and so real in what he does&#133; Here, he&#39;s a very dignified person who is extremely trusting and always full of positive hope&#133; You couldn&#39;t insult him if you try&#133; It&#39;s very hard to hurt his feelings&#133; He finds the bright side of every angle problem he faces and finds a way for him to live with the situation&#133; He has the virtue of patience, and the testament to hard work, perseverance, and humility&#133; He loves people, and he experiences the culture in an odd way&#133; <br /><br />Hanks plays a Krakozhian capable gentleman whose name is Viktor Navorski who finds himself without a passport and a visa once both are taken from him by the powers-that-be at the terminal, because his visa no longer counts, since his country is no longer in existence, and his passport is no longer valid&#133; <br /><br />Catherine Zeta-Jones brings vulnerability and insecurity to her unhappy character&#133; She plays the gorgeous flight attendant Amalia Warren, a very sensible woman who&#39;s always looking for love, trying to find the person that will be her prince&#133; She really wears her emotions on her sleeve and is lonely&#133; She wants some strong relationship in her life&#133; Viktor and Amelia have oceans of things in common and it ends up playing itself out and that&#39;s a nice thing to play&#133; <br /><br />The story leaves a lot for reflection, and in some ways, Viktor&#39;s stillness allows him to be a mirror for the people working in the airport to meditate on their own lives&#133; <br /><br />&quot;The Terminal&quot;
