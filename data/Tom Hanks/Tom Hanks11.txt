        <h4>Trivia</h4>


The character "Fillmore" was at one time to be named "Waldmire" after <a href="/name/nm2852388?ref_=tt_trv_trv">Bob Waldmire</a>, a self-proclaimed hippie artist known to Rt. 66 fans for his detailed pen-and-ink maps and postcards of the route. Though Waldmire's family owns the Cozy Dog Drive-In in Springfield, IL, Bob, now a vegan, preferred not have his name put on a character that would become a Happy Meal toy.        <a href="trivia?ref_=tt_trv_trv"
class="nobr" >See more</a>  &raquo;

                        <p itemprop="reviewBody">I went to the premier of &quot;Cars&quot; last night at the Lowe&#39;s Motor Speedway in Concorde and was delighted to find that the hours of traffic were worth it. <br /><br />The story has a clear point and the message of friendship is easily understood by kids and adults alike. The variety of humor suits all generations, too, making this the first &quot;summer must see&quot; for the family.<br /><br />Being an artist I was struck by the vibrancy of the animation. The colors were rich and beautiful, and there was such diverse vocal talent that the entire experience made it a treat for the crowd&#39;s eyes and ears. I&#39;ve never been to a movie with more than a few hundred people in the theater, but let me assure you that all 30,000+ attendees at the Speedway last night applauded during the credits and for some very, very good reasons.</p>
