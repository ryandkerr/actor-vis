Walter starts carrying a beaver puppet and tries to commit suicide (unsuccessfully). He uses the puppet to talk to himself, trying to bolster his spirits, and is trying to rebuild his life. Through the beaver, the family begins to learn about Walter's history and problems, and as he continues rebuilding, the beaver shows us all a way to cope.                <em class="nobr">Written by

This is a movie for all those people who want a second chance at setting things in their life right.<br /><br />Jodie looks beautiful as usual and the pair is as good as it was many years ago when they starred in &#x27;The Maverick&#x27;.
