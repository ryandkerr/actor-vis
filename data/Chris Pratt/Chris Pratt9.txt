I enjoyed that the movie showed the lives of these characters without necessarily trying to &#x22;fix&#x22; them. Many movies have recently tried to have a happy ending for all involved and in life that&#x27;s actually not the case. The actors where picked well for their roles. The downfall is the depth of the characters. While, I got the total picture, and during the scenes could feel the emotions for each person involved, such as the awkward scenes being convincingly awkward and the happy scenes genuinely making me happy. In the end I didn&#x27;t care what happens next. I probably would not buy this movie, unless it was on a super sale and would only leave it on t.v. if nothing else was on....</p>
