This is one of two movies released to cinemas in 2013 with the same premise: the garrisoning of the White House by criminals who hold the President hostage. The other is <a href="/title/tt2334879?ref_=tt_trv_trv">White House Down</a> (2013).        <a href="trivia?ref_=tt_trv_trv"

     <div id="goofs"  class="txt-block">
        <h4>Goofs</h4>
Kang uses the term &quot;Sea of Japan&quot;. Koreans (both North and South) use the term &quot;East Sea&quot;, as they find the term &quot;Sea of Japan&quot; offensive.
