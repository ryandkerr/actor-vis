Well-crafted comedy suffers from occasional lapses due to conventional story elements, but it&#x27;s good-natured and often laugh-out loud funny, with some terrific sight gags, clever ideas, and a perfect part for Carrey who makes a welcomed return to the kind of silliness that made him a star. ***</p>
